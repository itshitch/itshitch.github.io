About

I love to tell stories that words alone just don’t do justice. I’m a senior visual communications student at USC, and I capture stories through design, videography and photography. With every project, I find the angle that makes people stop, care and remember.

Currently, I’m the creative director at the Carolina Agency: a student-run, faculty-advised ad agency that creates deliverables for real businesses and nonprofits across the Southeast.

In 2026, I won a silver ADDY award in Publication Design, and more recently, I’ve had my work displayed on my parents’ fridge. When I’m not working on a project, you can find me playing ultimate frisbee, enjoying the weather outside, or hanging out with my cat, Fish.
